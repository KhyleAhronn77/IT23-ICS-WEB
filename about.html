About the College of Information Technology

Our Mission

Northern Bukidnon State College advances excellence in teaching, innovative research, and impactful community service through strategic partnerships, and inclusive and equitable education, empowering transformative development in Bukidnon and Region X.

Our Vision

A recognized institution for inclusive, culturally responsive, and sustainable higher education.

Core Values

Responsibility

Taking ownership and accountability of our actions and decisions, ensuring integrity and high standards across work, studies, and community engagements.

Adaptability

Embracing change, remaining flexible, and continuously adjusting to new challenges, environments, and opportunities.

Inclusivity

Creating an environment where every individual feels valued and respected, ensuring equal access to opportunities and resources.

Sustainability

Promoting practices that support long-term societal, environmental, and institutional well-being for future generations.

Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in performance, innovation, and productivity, ensuring outstanding outcomes across all endeavors.
